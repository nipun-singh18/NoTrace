# NoTrace 18.0: The Anti-Distraction Mystery

> **"In a world of 8-second attention spans, focus is the ultimate superpower."**

## The Mission
Modern social media has reduced human attention span to less than that of a goldfish. We scroll, we forget, and our minds become dull.

**NoTrace 18.0** is the antidote. It is a cinematic mystery experience designed to **recalibrate your focus**. It turns your device from a distraction into a tool for deduction, forcing players to **listen, observe, and analyze** to survive.

---

## The Experience

### 1. Cinematic Immersion
* **NIPUN PRODUCTIONS** presents a noir-style opening that sets a serious tone immediately.
* **Moral Weight:** Players must choose to "Be a Gentleman" to proceed.

### 2. Active Focus Training
* **Auditory Attention:** Real voice-acted clues (Mom, Sarah, Pizza Place) that require deep listening—no subtitles, no skipping.
* **Deductive Logic:** A realistic iOS Lock Screen where the password isn't given; it must be calculated from context clues.

### 3. The 3-Stage Protocol
* **Level 1 (Data):** Cross-reference Notes & Messages to crack the firewall.
* **Level 2 (Pattern):** A 6-Row "Brand Hunt" grid to train visual scanning (Vertical/Horizontal/Diagonal).
* **Level 3 (Reflex):** High-speed arcade evasion to test reaction time under pressure.

---

## Tech Stack
* **Core:** Swift 6.0 & SwiftUI
* **Audio:** AVFoundation (Layered background scores & voice cues)
* **Touch:** Custom DragGesture algorithms for smooth grid interaction.
* **Feel:** CoreHaptics for tactile immersion.

---

## How to Run
1.  Open NoTrace18.xcodeproj in Xcode.
2.  **Crucial:** Ensure sarah.mp3, mom.mp3, etc., have **Target Membership** checked.
3.  Run on **iPhone 17 Pro Simulator**.
4.  *Sound On is mandatory.*
